FAQs About SJU

Q: Where is SJU located?
A: SJU is located at 5600 City Ave, Philadelphia PA
Q: Is SJU a pretty school?
A: Yes! SJU has many unique buildings. Barbelin is one of the most photographed, being made of stone and on the main campus. You can't miss it!
Q: What kind of programs does SJU offer?
A: There are two schools at SJU: Business and College of Arts and Sciences. The Business school ranges from Business Management to Food Marketing, and the College of Arts and Sciences ranges from Communications to Education.
Q: What is the magis?
A: Saint Joe's is affiliated with the Jesuits. The MAGIS principle can be defined as: live greater, be your best, outshine yourself. We offer many service activities at the university to satisfy the MAGIS, as well as other opportunities.
Q: What makes SJU so great?
A: Lots of things! Our campus is full of pride, spirit, service, and friendship. The classes are challenging and rewarding. There are tons of clubs and activities for everyone's hobbies. Service is huge, and the sense of community is overwhelming.
