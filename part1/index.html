FAQs About SJU

Q: Where is SJU located?
A: SJU is located at 5600 City Ave, Philadelphia PA
Q: Is SJU a pretty school?
A: Yes! SJU has many unique buildings. Barbelin is one of the most photographed, being made of stone and on the main campus. You can't miss it!
Q: What kind of programs does SJU offer?
A: There are two schools at SJU: Business and College of Arts and Sciences. The Business school ranges from Business Management to Food Marketing, and the College of Arts and Sciences ranges from Communications to Education.
